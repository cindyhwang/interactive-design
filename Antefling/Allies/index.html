allies
Based in Long Island, Allies blends the flavors of funk and R&B with an experimental rock twist. They self-released their debut EP, The Lobby, in Fall 2014. With nods to the feel-good rhythms of Stevie Wonder, far out noises of Pink Floyd, and smooth approach of Marvin Gaye, their unorthodox sound is not easily pinpointed … and certainly won't be soon forgotten. Earth is their groove guild, and all are welcome.
