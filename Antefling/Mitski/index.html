Since releasing her debut album Bury Me at Makeout Creek in 2014, Mitski has received international acclaim for her distinct, arresting sound and profoundly reflective lyrics. Pitchfork applauded the release as "inventive and resourceful," while Rolling Stone celebrated her "deep-cutting lyrics." Mitski has also received widespread attention for her "cathartic" live shows, as dubbed by The New York Times' Jon Caramanica. In 2015, Mitski has continued delivering her particular flavor of soul-baring rock, touring throughout North America and beyond.
