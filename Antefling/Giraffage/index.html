giraffage
Giraffage is San Francisco-based producer and beat guru, Charlie Yin. His signature sound of electronic pop has won him praise from Pitchfork, FADER and XLR8R. He broke into the music scene with his debut EP, Pretty Things, in 2011. It was soon followed by his synth-pop debut album, Comfort, which XLR8R called an “ebullient slice of electronic pop … [with] pleasantly ambient and sample-laden instrumentals.” He most recently released the EP No Reason via Fool's Gold, in November 2014.
