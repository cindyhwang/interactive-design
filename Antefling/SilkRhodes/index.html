silk rhodes
Hailing from Baltimore, Silk Rhodes are astro-traveling producer Michael Collins and singer Sasha Desree. Their voyage began in a 1997 Honda CRV turned DIY recording studio. Collins and Desreem would broadcast their experiments to the streets, commenting on their observations in rhyme and soliciting ideas from strangers. These experiments spawned sound collages and stream-of-consciousness hymns, as well as songs that would appear on their 2014 self-titled debut, released by Stones Throw.
