giraffage
Giraffage is San Francisco-based producer and beat guru, Charlie Yin. His signature sound of electronic pop has won him praise from Pitchfork, FADER and XLR8R. He burst onto the music scene with his debut, Pretty Things EP. It was followed by his synth-pop debut album, Comfort, which XLR8R called "[an] ebullient slice of electronic pop...[with] pleasantly ambient and sample-laden instrumentals." He most recently dropped his No Reason EP via Fool's Gold. He has toured with Phantogram and Flume.
