This project renders Michael Rock's 2009 essay "Fuck Content" in 20 different styles. Fifteen were copied from existing websites, four were self-designed, and one is a mashup of two existing web styles. I copied either websites that I frequent, or that I thought treated long-form text in formally interesting ways. My emphasis here was on legibility, hence the large number of news and journal websites.
